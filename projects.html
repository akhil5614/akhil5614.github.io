With the fast development of the world aerospace, the design and manufacture of landing gear are becoming hot topics nowadays.

Solar Powered Aircraft

The association of renewable energy with our present technology is a very vital step in battling global warming and facing the energy crisis.

Hovercraft

A hovercraft, also known as an air-cushion vehicle or ACV, is a craft capable of travelling over land, water, mud, ice, and other surfaces.

Autonomous Aircraft

An unmanned aircraft that does not allow pilot intervention in the management of the flight.

Ornithoper

An ornithopter is an aircraft that flies by flapping its wings. It is designed by imitating the flapping wing flight of birds.

RC Boat

Radio controlled (or remote controlled) boats are controlled remotely using radio control equipments.

Autogyro

An autogyro also known as a gyroplane or gyrocopter, is a type of rotorcraft that uses an unpowered rotor in free autorotation to develop lift.

Tricopter

A tricopter is similar to a helicopter except that it has three vertical rotors as exposed to a heli's one ( or two in the case of coaxial).
